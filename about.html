About StreamSlot

StreamSlot is a free, browser-based dashboard designed specifically for trading card box breakers who stream live on platforms like Whatnot, eBay Live, TikTok, and YouTube.

What is Card Breaking?

Card breaking (or "box breaking") is when a host opens sealed trading card boxes live on stream and distributes the cards to participants who purchased teams or slots. StreamSlot makes this process smooth and professional.

Key Features

  • OBS-Ready Scene: Chroma key support for transparent green screen backgrounds
  • Multiple Sports: Pre-loaded NBA (30 teams), MLB (30 teams), NFL (32 teams)
  • Custom Slots: Create your own slot names with custom colors
  • Team Randomizer: Fair random team selection with animated overlay
  • Trade Machine: Easy team swaps and respins between buyers
  • Transaction Log: Track all purchases, trades, and respins with timestamps
  • CSV Export: Export buyer assignments for easy shipping
  • Stream Overlays: Full-screen animations for engagement (Live @ $1, PYT, etc.)
  • Auto-Save: All data persists in your browser automatically

100% Free & Privacy-Focused

StreamSlot is completely free to use. All your data stays in your browser - we don't collect or store anything on servers. No signup required, no hidden fees.

Open Source

StreamSlot is open source and available on GitHub. Contributions and feedback are welcome!
