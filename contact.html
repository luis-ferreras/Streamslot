Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my board data?

StreamSlot automatically saves all your data to your browser's local storage. Your teams, buyers, and settings persist between sessions. You can also export backups from the Export tab.

Can I use StreamSlot on multiple devices?

Yes! Use the Export tab to download your board data as a backup file, then import it on another device. Your data stays private and never touches our servers.

Is StreamSlot really free?

Yes, StreamSlot is 100% free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. It's an open-source project built for the card breaking community.

How do I add StreamSlot to OBS?

Add a Browser Source in OBS pointing to streamslot.app. Enable the green screen background in the Scene tab, then use a Chroma Key filter in OBS to make the background transparent.

My data disappeared. What happened?

Data is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data, using private/incognito mode, or switching browsers will result in a fresh start. Always export backups regularly from the Export tab.

Response Time

StreamSlot is maintained as a community project. We review GitHub issues regularly and aim to respond within a few days. Critical bugs are prioritized.
